BREAKING NEWS: Boston Celtics Win 2024 NBA Finals

June 17, 2024

BOSTON — The Boston Celtics have officially won the 2024 NBA Finals! They defeated the Dallas Mavericks in Game 5 with a decisive 106-88 victory. 

This historic win secures the Celtics' 18th NBA Championship, breaking their tie with the Los Angeles Lakers for the most titles in NBA history. Jaylen Brown was named the Finals MVP after an incredible performance throughout the series. 

The city of Boston is celebrating this monumental achievement!
